1. Policy Overview

Grindpoint Nigeria Limited ("we," "us," "our") is committed to ensuring that every legitimate Naira earned on our platform reaches you as quickly, securely, and transparently as possible.

This Payout Policy forms part of our overall Terms & Conditions and applies to all registered users of the Grindpoint platform. By requesting a withdrawal, you confirm that you have read and agreed to this policy in full.

All withdrawals are paid in Nigerian Naira (₦) only. We do not offer payments in any foreign currency, cryptocurrency, or gift cards.

2. Minimum Withdrawal Threshold

The minimum amount you may withdraw from your Grindpoint wallet at any one time is:

Minimum Withdrawal
₦1,000
One thousand Naira

Balances below ₦1,000 are not eligible for withdrawal until the threshold is reached. The maximum single withdrawal per transaction is your full available balance.

We reserve the right to adjust the minimum withdrawal threshold. Any changes will be communicated via platform announcement at least 7 days before taking effect.

4. Withdrawal Process

Follow these steps to request a withdrawal:

1
Log in and visit your Dashboard

Navigate to your dashboard at grindpoint.ng/dashboard.html and ensure your balance is ₦1,000 or above.

2
Click "Withdraw Funds"

Press the "Withdraw Funds" button on your dashboard to open the withdrawal form.

3
Select your payment method

Choose from Bank Transfer, OPay/Palmpay, or Kuda Bank tabs and enter your account details carefully.

4
Enter the withdrawal amount

Type the amount you wish to withdraw (minimum ₦1,000, maximum your full available balance).

5
Submit your request

Review your details and click "Submit Request." Your request enters our admin review queue.

6
Admin review & payment

Our team reviews and processes your request within 1–2 business days. Funds are sent directly to your provided account.

5. Processing Times

All withdrawal requests are reviewed by our admin team before being processed. Processing times are as follows:

  • Standard requests (₦1,000–₦49,999): 1 business day
  • Large requests (₦50,000–₦199,999): 1–2 business days
  • High-value requests (₦200,000+): 2–3 business days (may require additional identity verification)

Business days are Monday through Friday, excluding Nigerian public holidays. Requests submitted after 4:00 PM WAT or on weekends/holidays will be reviewed on the next business day.

We aim to process all requests promptly. However, factors such as bank downtime, public holidays, or unusual transaction volumes may occasionally cause delays beyond these estimates.

6. Fees & Charges

Grindpoint does not charge any withdrawal fee on our end. The full amount you request will be sent to your bank or wallet.

However, please note:

  • Your bank or mobile wallet provider may deduct a standard transfer/receiving fee in accordance with their own policies. Grindpoint has no control over these charges.
  • Some mobile wallets may have daily or monthly receiving limits that could affect when you see the funds in your wallet.

7. Identity Verification

To protect our users and comply with Nigerian financial regulations, we may require identity verification before processing certain withdrawals. This may include:

  • Confirmation of your registered phone number via OTP
  • Submission of a government-issued ID (National ID, Voter's Card, Driver's Licence, or International Passport)
  • BVN (Bank Verification Number) verification for large withdrawal requests
  • Selfie/photo verification for high-risk or first-time large withdrawals

We will contact you at your registered email address if additional verification is required. Failure to provide requested verification within 7 days may result in the withdrawal request being cancelled.

9. Failed or Returned Payments

If a payment fails or is returned due to:

  • Incorrect account number or account name provided
  • Account closed or dormant
  • Daily/monthly receiving limits on your wallet
  • Bank system downtime

The funds will be returned to your Grindpoint wallet within 3–5 business days. You will be notified via email and can submit a corrected withdrawal request.

Important: Grindpoint is not liable for failed payments resulting from incorrect account details provided by you. Please double-check all payment details before submitting a withdrawal request.

10. Payout Disputes

If you believe a withdrawal has been incorrectly processed, not received, or you have any concern about a payment:

  1. Wait at least 3 business days from the approval date before raising a dispute (to account for standard bank transfer delays).
  2. Check with your bank or wallet provider to confirm the transaction has not been received.
  3. Contact us at support@grindpoint.ng with:
    • Your registered email address
    • The withdrawal amount and date requested
    • Your bank/wallet name and account number
    • Any reference number provided

Our team will investigate and respond within 5 business days. Where a payment error is confirmed on our end, we will reprocess the payment or credit your wallet promptly.

11. Balance Forfeiture

Your wallet balance may be forfeited (set to zero without payout) in the following circumstances:

  • Account suspension or permanent ban due to violation of our Terms & Conditions (fraud, multi-accounting, task abuse, etc.)
  • Voluntary account deletion with a balance below ₦1,000
  • Account inactivity for more than 12 consecutive months with no tasks completed and no login. We will send a 30-day advance notice email before forfeiting an inactive balance.
  • Verified fraudulent earning activity (e.g., automated task completion, fake referrals)

Forfeited balances are not refundable and cannot be appealed in the case of Terms violations confirmed by our investigation.

12. Contact & Support

For all payout-related inquiries:

  • Email: support@grindpoint.ng
  • Response time: Within 1–2 business days
  • Hours: Monday – Friday, 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM WAT

Please include your registered email address and account details in all support communications to help us assist you faster.
